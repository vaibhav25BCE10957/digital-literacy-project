Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1 Always check website URL before entering details
2 Never share OTP with anyone
3 Use strong and unique passwords
4 Enable two factor authentication
5 Do not click suspicious links
6 Avoid using public WiFi for banking
7 Download apps only from official stores
8 Regularly update your software
9 Report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in
10 Call helpline 1930 for financial fraud
